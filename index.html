Bio & Research Interest
I'm now a Research Fellow@NANYANG TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY and working in the research group of Prof. Liu Yang (刘杨). I'm also the co-learder of the Deep Reinforcement Learning Lab , founded by Prof. Jianye Hao (郝建业) in Tianjin University. My research concerns many different aspects of Artificial Intelligence, Deep Reinforcement Learning, and Multiagent System, especially the opponent modeling topic, including behavior analytics, prolicy representation and interpretation in multiagent problems.
