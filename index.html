Bio. I'm now a Research Fellow at NANYANG TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY and also a member of the DEEP REINFORCEMENT LEARNING LAB in Tianjin University. I received my Ph.D degree from the COLLEGE OF INTELLIGENT AND COMPUTING, Tianjin University, where I worked with Prof.Jianye Hao on opponent modeling in multiagent system and their applications in Deep Reinforcement Learning. I also collaborated with Fuxi AI Lab, Netease, Inc., in designing intelligent and diverse Game AIs.

My research concerns many different aspects of Artificial Intelligence, Deep Reinforcement Learning, and Multiagent System, especially the opponent modeling topics, including behavior analytics, rolicy representation and interpretation in multiaget problems.
